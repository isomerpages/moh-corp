<h2><strong>Stay active in your golden years with Age Well SG</strong></h2><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/happy_asian_senior_couple_blue_pink_shirt_laughing_while_riding_bicycles_min.jpg"></div><p>Age Well SG is a national programme by MOH, MND and MOT to support seniors to age well in their homes and their communities. It aims to tackle social isolation, which is a key cause of frailty, and empower seniors to age actively.</p><p>Ageing in the community is a holistic approach that emphasises the importance of creating supportive environments for older individuals to thrive within their neighbourhoods.</p><p>It involves fostering community connections, providing accessible infrastructure, and offering tailored services to meet the diverse needs of seniors.</p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/aged_couple_resting_park_min.jpg"></div><p>By promoting social inclusion, age-friendly housing, and convenient healthcare access, communities can empower their older residents to maintain independence and actively participate in civic life</p><hr><h3><strong>Preventing frailty</strong></h3><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/help_care_asian_senior_elderly_old_lady_woman_use_walker_with_strong_health_while_walking_park_happy_fresh_holiday_min.jpg"></div><p>Seniors who age well in the community can lower their risk of social isolation, and prevent deterioration of health.</p><p>Preventing frailty among seniors involves a multifaceted approach that addresses physical, mental, and social well-being. Encouraging social engagement and creating age-friendly environments can contribute to maintaining physical and mental resilience. These measures collectively support seniors in leading healthier and more active lives as they age.</p><hr><p></p>
