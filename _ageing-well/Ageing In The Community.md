<h2><strong>Stay active in your golden years with Age Well SG</strong></h2><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/happy_asian_senior_couple_blue_pink_shirt_laughing_while_riding_bicycles_min.jpg"></div><p><a href="https://www.agewellsg.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Age Well SG</a> is a national programme by MOH, MND and MOT to support seniors to age well in their homes and their communities. It aims to tackle social isolation, which is a key cause of frailty, and empower seniors to age actively.</p><hr><h3><strong>Active Ageing</strong></h3><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/aged_couple_resting_park_min.jpg"></div><p>Active aging is about enjoying a better and longer quality of life! Incorporating these habits into your lifestyle can contribute to a healthier and more fulfilling aging process:</p><ol data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>Move regularly:</strong> Include aerobic, strength, and flexibility exercises.</p></li><li><p><strong>Stay mentally active:</strong> Read, solve puzzles, learn new skills.</p></li><li><p><strong>Connect socially:</strong> Stay engaged with friends, family, and the community.</p></li><li><p><strong>Eat well:</strong> Maintain a balanced, nutritious diet.</p></li><li><p><strong>Get enough sleep:</strong> Get at least 7 to 8 hours of sleep daily.</p></li><li><p><strong>Go for regular health screenings: </strong><a href="https://www.healthhub.sg/programmes/screen_for_life?utm_source=google&amp;utm_medium=sem&amp;utm_campaign=fy23-screen-for-life&amp;utm_content=rsa-generic-1&amp;gclid=Cj0KCQiAh8OtBhCQARIsAIkWb6_qnE-BEIEPj680Fm7MsxR7_SLDKq0G9IraaicNdYqVDty056nUYe8aArq4EALw_wcB" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Screen for Life - National Health Screening Programme</a></p></li><li><p><strong>Preventive Measures:</strong> Stay updated on vaccinations and screenings.</p></li></ol><h4>Active Ageing Centres</h4><div class="isomer-card-grid"><a rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" href="https://www.go.gov.sg/aacgowhere" class="isomer-card"><div class="isomer-card-image"><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Placeholder image" src="/images/AAC.png"></div></div><div class="isomer-card-body"><div class="isomer-card-title">Active Ageing Centres</div><div class="isomer-card-description">Engage in activities, communal meals, and foster social interactions to reduce social isolation.</div><div class="isomer-card-link">Locate the AAC nearest to you.</div></div></a></div><p>AACs are suitable for all seniors who would like to remain physically, mentally and socially active. They offer a range of activities and programmes for seniors with varied interests and abilities.</p><p></p><hr><h3><strong>Preventing frailty</strong></h3><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/help_care_asian_senior_elderly_old_lady_woman_use_walker_with_strong_health_while_walking_park_happy_fresh_holiday_min.jpg"></div><p>Preventing frailty is all about staying active, eating well, and keeping your mind sharp.</p><p>By embracing these habits, you'll boost your resilience and enjoy a more independent and vibrant life as you age!</p><hr><p></p>
