<h4>Stay Active in your golden years with Age Well SG</h4><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/athletic_asian_senior_woman_running_outdoor_jogging_park_min.jpg"></div><p><strong><a href="https://www.agewellsg.gov.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Age Well SG</a> </strong>is a national programme by MOH, MND and MOT to support seniors to age well in their homes and their communities. It aims to tackle social isolation, which is a key cause of frailty, and empower seniors to age actively.</p><p>Ageing in the community is a holistic approach that emphasises the importance of creating supportive environments for older individuals to thrive within their neighbourhoods.</p><p>It involves fostering community connections, providing accessible infrastructure, and offering tailored services to meet the diverse needs of seniors.</p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/help_care_asian_senior_elderly_old_lady_woman_use_walker_with_strong_health_while_walking_park_happy_fresh_holiday_min.jpg"></div><p>By promoting social inclusion, age-friendly housing, and convenient healthcare access, communities can empower their older residents to maintain independence and actively participate in civic life</p><hr><h4>Preventing frailty</h4><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/senior_asian_woman_body_warming_before_exercising_old_woman_stretching_before_jogging_garden_sport_athlete_running_concept_min.jpg"></div><h4></h4><h4>Providing care and support to our seniors</h4><p>Your loved ones may fall sick and need medical help or receive care as they recover at home.
