<p>Healthier SG is a national initiative by the Ministry of Health (MOH)
that focuses on preventive care.</p>

<p>By enrolling, you can take proactive steps to manage your health, prevent
the onset of chronic diseases, and have strong support to lead healthier

<h3><strong>Ways to live healthy</strong></h3>
<h4><strong>Preventive care</strong></h4>
<p>Preventive care plays a crucial role in maintaining and promoting your
overall health and well-being. Its importance lies in your proactive approach
to identify and address potential health issues before they become serious.</p>

<h4><strong>Staying informed</strong></h4>
<p>Being aware of your health condition and finding out about different health
tips can empower you to take proactive steps to foster overall well-being
and lead you to a healthier lifestyle.</p>
