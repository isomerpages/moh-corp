<h2><strong>Financial support for long-term care</strong></h2><p>Find out how these CPF schemes can help you finance your long-term care needs.</p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/doctor_take_care_support_woman_patient_sitting_wheelchair_health_care_insurance_concept.jpg"></div><ol data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>CareShield Life</strong></p></li></ol><p>CareShield Life is a long-term care insurance. You can benefit from monthly cash payouts as long as you are unable to do 3 or more Activities of Daily Living (ADLs).</p><p><a href="https://www.cpf.gov.sg/member/healthcare-financing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Find out more</u></a></p><ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>ElderShield</strong></p></li></ol><p>If you are covered under ElderShield, you can benefit from monthly cash payouts of up to $400, for up to six years in the event of severe disability.</p><p><a href="https://www.cpf.gov.sg/member/healthcare-financing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Find out more</u></a></p><ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>MediSave Care</strong></p></li></ol><p>If you’re aged 30 and above with severe disability and require long-term care, you can withdraw up to $200 per month from your own, or your spouse’s MediSave.</p><p><a href="https://www.cpf.gov.sg/member/healthcare-financing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Find out more</u></a></p><ol start="4" data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>MediSave</strong></p></li></ol><p>You can use your MediSave to cover part of your palliative care and rehabilitation treatments expenses.</p><p><a href="https://www.cpf.gov.sg/member/healthcare-financing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Find out more</u></a></p><ol start="5" data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p><strong>MediShield Life</strong></p></li></ol><p>MediShield Life is a health insurance that can help you&nbsp;with large palliative care and rehabilitation treatment bills.</p><p><a href="https://www.cpf.gov.sg/member/healthcare-financing" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>Find out more</u></a></p>
