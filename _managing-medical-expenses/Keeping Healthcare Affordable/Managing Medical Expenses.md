<h3><strong>Subsidies, MediSave, MediShield Life and MediFund (S+3Ms)</strong></h3>
<p>Singapore's public healthcare policy is anchored on the philosophy of
individual responsibility and affordable healthcare for all.</p>
<p>Our healthcare system provides universal healthcare coverage to all citizens,
formed by the S+3Ms system.</p>
<h3><strong>Get to know these schemes</strong></h3>

<div class="isomer-card-title">MediShield Life</div>
<div class="isomer-card-description">MediShield Life is a basic health insurance plan, administered by the
Central Provident Fund (CPF) board, which helps to pay for large hospital
bills and selected costly outpatient treatments. It is structured so that
patients pay less Medisave/Cash for large hospital bills.</div>
