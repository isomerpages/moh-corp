<h2>What is MediShield Life?</h2><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/life_insurance_concept_with_stethoscope.jpg"></div><p>MediShield Life is a basic health insurance plan, administered by the Central Provident Fund (CPF) Board, which helps to pay for large hospital bills and selected costly outpatient treatments, such as dialysis and chemotherapy for cancer. It is structured so that patients pay less MediSave/cash for large hospital bills.</p><h3><strong>Who Is Eligible?</strong></h3><p>All Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents, including the very old and those who have pre-existing conditions.<br><br>Please click <a href="https://www.moh.gov.sg/home/our-healthcare-system/medishield-life/what-is-medishield-life/medishield-life" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> for more information.</p><h3><strong>What Are The Benefits?</strong></h3><p>MediShield Life is sized for subsidised treatment in public hospitals and pegged at B2/C-type wards. If you choose to stay in an A/B1-type ward or in a&nbsp;private hospital, you are still covered by MediShield Life. However, you will find that your MediShield Life payout will cover only a small proportion of your bill. You would need to draw from MediSave and/or cash to pay the balance.<br><br>Please click <a href="https://www.moh.gov.sg/home/our-healthcare-system/medishield-life/what-is-medishield-life/how-to-make-a-medishield-life-claim" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> or more information on how to make a MediShield Life Claim.<br><br>Please click <a href="https://www.moh.gov.sg/home/our-healthcare-system/medishield-life/what-is-medishield-life/what-medishield-life-benefits" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to read more on MediShield Life Benefits.</p><h3><strong>How Can I Enhance MediShield Life Coverage?</strong></h3><p>If you plan to use an A/B1-type ward in a public hospital or go to a private hospital for your future hospitalisations, you can consider purchasing a&nbsp;MediSave-approved private Integrated Shield Plan (IP), an insurance plan that comprises:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>MediShield Life</p></li><li><p>Private insurance coverage providing additional benefits and coverage (e.g. to cover the costs of private hospitals or A/B1-type wards in the public hospitals).</p></li></ul><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" title="MediShield Life" alt="MediShield Life" src="https://www.moh.gov.sg/images/librariesprovider5/default-album/medishield-life.png?sfvrsn=7e8c3f8b_0"></div><p><br>If you have an IP, you are already covered by MediShield Life. The premiums you pay to your private insurer already include the premiums for the MediShield Life component.<br><br>Please click <a href="https://www.moh.gov.sg/healthcare-schemes-subsidies/medishield-life/about-integrated-shield-plans" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> for more information.</p><p></p>
